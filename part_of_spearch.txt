A pronoun is a part of speech used to ( местоимение ) - avoid repetition of a noun.

A noun is a part of speech used to ( существительное ) - name a thing or a person

A verb is a part of speech used to  ( глагол ) - express action

An adjective is a part of speech used to ( прилагательное ) - modify a noun or a pronoun by describing a particular quality of a noun or a pronoun they are modifying.

An adverb is a part of speech used to ( наречие ) - modify a verb, other adverbs or adjectives.

A conjunction is a part of speech used to ( предлог ) - put words, phrases or parts of the sentence together.

A preposition is a part of speech used to ( союз ) - show the relationship between a noun or pronoun and another part of a sentence.
